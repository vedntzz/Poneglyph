## Minutes of Meeting — Q1 Progress Review

Meeting convened at 11:00 AM at the District Collector's office, Sagar. DC opened by noting that the FPC program is a priority under the state's agricultural transformation agenda.

### Key Discussion Points

**FPC Registration Progress:**
- 8 of 12 planned FPCs have been registered with MCA. Remaining 4 are in documentation stage.
- DC suggested increasing the target to 15 given demand from Tikamgarh and Panna districts where farmer groups have self-organized and are requesting formal FPC status.
- Project Director agreed, noting this would require a minor logframe amendment but is within budget.

**Infrastructure:**
- Sagar cold storage: land allotted, contractor selected (M/s Sharma Constructions). Foundation work to begin by November 15.
- Damoh sale point: land allotment still pending. Revenue department has not responded to the application filed in August. DC offered to write personally to DC Damoh.

**Capacity Building:**
- 12 PHM trainings completed so far (8 general, 4 women-specific).
- BDO Rahatgarh raised concern about low attendance in Banda block — suggested scheduling trainings after harvest season.

### Action Items
1. **Vikram Mehta:** Submit logframe amendment for FPC target (12→15) to World Bank by October 31
2. **DC Sagar:** Write to DC Damoh regarding sale point land allotment
3. **All block coordinators:** Begin weekly WhatsApp updates from October 25

### Tone Note
Meeting was constructive. DC is clearly supportive of the project. The FPC target increase was her idea, which suggests genuine institutional buy-in rather than just compliance.
