This commitment was made after the project team flagged that ev-003 (WhatsApp claim of 32 women trained in Hatta) could not be verified without supporting documentation.

**What's needed:**
- Signed attendance sheets for all women's PHM trainings conducted in Damoh district (at least 4 trainings claimed so far)
- Each sheet must have participant names, signatures/thumbprints, and the trainer's signature
- Geotagged photos from training venues (promised in WhatsApp but never delivered)

**Why it matters:**
Output 3.2 (Women's PHM trainings) is a gender-specific indicator that the World Bank monitors closely. Without attendance documentation, these trainings cannot be counted in the Q2 report. The Drafter will mark these as "unverifiable" and the Auditor will tag them ⚠ (contested).

**Escalation plan:**
If not received by December 22, the Project Manager will raise this in the January district review meeting and request the BDO's office to independently verify training records.
